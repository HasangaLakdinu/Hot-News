# Hot-News
Hot News is a android app that gives you the Hottest News  all around the World
Users has to Choose One category On their personal demand
Categories Are <br>
1.Espn Cricinfo<br>
2.CNN News<br>
3.Verge News<br>
4.National Geographic News<br>
5.Al Jazeera<br>
6.ESPN News<br>
7.Google News<br>
8.BBC News
